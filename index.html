About Light of African Child Uganda

"10 Years of hope and education for unprivileged children"

Our Story

For a decade, we have been rooted in Kampala, Uganda, building hope and providing education for underprivileged children. What started as a local effort to provide school supplies has grown into a comprehensive support network dedicated to transforming lives.

Our journey is driven by the belief that every child, regardless of their background, deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential. Through community-led initiatives, we provide more than just basic needs; we offer a path to self-reliance and dignity.

Our Vision

To see a country where orphans and underprivileged children are accorded hope for a better life and future.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for orphans and underprivileged children in Uganda by providing a home, education, and other basic needs.

Core Values

Equity, Transparency, Accountability, Integrity, and Professionalism.
