About Light of African Child Uganda

"10 Years of hope and education for unprivileged children"

Our Story

For a decade, we have been rooted in Kampala, Uganda, building hope and providing education for underprivileged children. What started as a local effort to provide school supplies has grown into a comprehensive support network dedicated to transforming lives.

Our Vision

To see a country where orphans and underprivileged children are accorded hope for a better life and future.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for orphans and underprivileged children in Uganda by providing a home, education, and other basic needs.

Core Values

Equity, Transparency, Accountability, Integrity, and Professionalism.

Featured Story: "Jamilah's Journey"

Meet Jamilah, an inspiring young lady who has defied the odds. Despite living with a disability, her strength and dedication have become the heart of our mission.

Jamilah's resilience reminds us every day why we do what we do. Her journey from struggle to empowerment is a testament to the impact of community support and vocational training.

Our Programs

Community Education

Quality education as a catalyst for change.

  • Impact: 100 Students Supported; 92% Graduation Rate.
  • Features: School fees, uniforms, and educational supplies.

Safe Shelter Project

Secure, family-style residential facilities for orphaned children.

  • Impact: 8 Shelters Built; 180 Children Housed.
  • Features: 24 Trained caregivers in a loving environment.

Skill Development & Vocational Training

Practical training to foster self-reliance and economic independence.

  • What We Do: Tailoring, craftsmanship, and technical skills.
  • Inclusion: Accessible programs for individuals like Jamilah.

Health Awareness Campaign

Protecting our communities through preventative education.

  • Impact: 2,500 Screenings; 150+ Consultations in 2025.
  • Initiatives: HIV/AIDS, Malaria prevention, vaccinations.

Food & Nutrition Program

Ensuring every child has access to healthy, balanced meals.

  • Focus: Daily balanced meals and nutritional supplements.
  • Outcome: Improved health and focus for 100+ children.

Community Talent Development

Nurturing the spirit through traditional dance, music, and sports.

  • Impact: 350 Talents Nurtured; 24 Performances.

Environmental Conservation

Protecting resources through tree planting and sustainable agriculture.

  • Impact: 5,000 Trees Planted; 450 Families Involved.

Anti-Discrimination Initiative

Creating inclusive, accepting communities. We fight against all forms of discrimination.

  • Advocacy: 85 Advocates trained to champion equality.
